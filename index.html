About Me

I am a Data Scientist at Fidelity Investments in Boston, MA. My work is focused on making LLM systems reliable, robust, and trustworthy. My day could look anything from finetuning LLMs to enhance their reasoning capabilities to architecting agentic workflows to streamline enterprise workflows. I hold a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Massachusetts Amherst, and a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad.

During my master's journey, I collaborated as a Graduate Research Extern with teams at Google DeepMind and IBM Research (through the Information Extraction and Synthesis (IESL) Lab and 696DS Industry Mentorship Program). My research explored advanced ensembling techniques, and llm alignment methods using self-play optimizations and distribution matching techniques.

I took classes such as CS 687 - Reinforcement Learning taught by Prof. Bruno Silva and CS 685 - Advanded Natural Language Processing by Prof. Mohit Iyyer, that left a great impact on me in terms of appreciating the nuances of these fields.

Prior to my master's, I worked as an MLE at Express Analytics, building ML pipelines for Marketing and Customer Analytics, where I implemented DL solutions for Multi-Touch-Attribution and Marketing-Mix-Models
